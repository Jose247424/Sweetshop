When we started out as a small tuck shop in 1949, next to the Thanjavur Railway Station, little did we know what a big hit it would be amongst our customers! The seeds were first sown by our Founder, a pioneer in taste- Sri Gurudhayal Sharma and ever since then, there has been no looking back.

Serving our customers has always been our only dream. We
are passionate about making lip- smacking delicious food
with one sole aim- to cook and serve with honesty from the
heart. And through these seventy years, if there is one thing
that hasn't changed, it has to be our inimitable authenticity
and our promise to deliver excellence in every bite. Bombay
Sweets has always been famous for its unparalleled quality
and we hope to revolutionise serving flavoursome food with
the highest standards of quality and hygiene in the food
industry across India! For us, our purpose goes beyond food.
It is about the relationship we build with every one of our
customers! Which is why, our tagline is- ‘The Taste of Bond’.
