ABOUT US
History of the Brand

When we started out as a small tuck shop in 1949,
next to the Thanjavur Railway Station, little did we know
what a big hit it would be amongst our customers!
The seeds were first sown by our Founder, a pioneer in taste- Sri Gurudhayal
Sharma and ever since then, there has been no looking back.
It has been an exhilarating journey of over 70 years of serving the best of
our innovations, finely crafted recipes and we always thank the almighty for
giving us the opportunity to reach countless number of people through our
mutual love for food! Today, we are proud!Today,
we are proud to say that thanks to our ever-growing loyal customer support and love,
we are now 14 branches strong with bases in Tanjore and Pattukottai.
We are proud to say irrespective of a festival or not,
people still throng to our stores because of the unmatched quality,
delectable taste and how every morsel evokes a sense of nostalgia!
